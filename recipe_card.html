Banana cake

Ingredients

  • 175g flour
  • 140g butter
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 175g dark chocolate
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 170 fan and line the baking tin
  2. Whisk the butter and sugar together in a bowl till the mixture reaches a light brown color
  3. Slowly add the two eggs and mix
  4. Sive through the flour to the mixture and fold
  5. Add the mashed bananas to the bowl with a teaspoon of vanilla extract
  6. Add the chopped dark chocolate to the bowl and stir
  7. Add the mixture to the baking tin and bake in the oven for 50mins-1hr
